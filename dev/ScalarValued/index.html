Circular Law

RandomMatrix.Circular — Type
Circular(c=0,R=1)
  • The uniform distribution on the complex disk with center c and radius R
rand(Circular(1,10),200)

Generates 200 uniform r.v.s on the complex disk of center 1 radius 10.
